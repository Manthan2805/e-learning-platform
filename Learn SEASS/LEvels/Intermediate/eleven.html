Thetext-align property specifies the horizontal alignment of text in an element.

Property Values:

  • rotateX: Rotates towards the x-axis.
  • rotateY: Rotates towards the y-axis.
  • rotateZ: Rotates towards the z-axis.
  • initial: Sets this property to its default value..
  • inherit: Inherits this property from its parent element.

For example, transform: rotateX(150deg); will align the text to center
